Frequently Asked Questions

Is $35.55 really the full price for an oil change?

Yes. $35.55 is our everyday, all-in price for a full-synthetic oil change. That includes up to 5 quarts of full-synthetic oil, a genuine Toyota oil filter, a multi-point inspection, and all taxes and fees. No coupon required.

Do I need an appointment for service?

We accept walk-ins, but scheduling an appointment helps us get you in and out faster. You can book online or call us at (954) 448-7257.

Can I get financing if I have bad credit?

Yes. We work with 20+ lenders and specialize in all credit situations.

Where is Toyota of Coconut Creek located?

We're at 5201 W Sample Rd, Coconut Creek, FL 33073, right on Sample Road, minutes from Coral Springs, Pompano Beach, and Boca Raton.

Do you have Spanish-speaking staff?

Absolutely. Our team speaks English, Spanish, Portuguese, and Haitian Creole.
